Ordered list

These lists use numbers to form oulines
(The kind of numeral is controlled with CSS.)

  1. needs
  2. wants

You can tell it what number to start on:

  1. School
  2. World travel
  3. Job

You can force an order with specific values:

  1. Bottles of beer on the wall
  2. Bottles of beer on the wall
  3. Bottles of beer on the wall

Lists can be multilayered:

  1. Friends
  2. Family
    1. Parents
      1. Mom
      2. Dad
    2. Siblings
      1. Brothers
        1. Tom
        2. Dick
        3. Harry
      2. Sisters
        1. Kourteny
        2. Kim
        3. Khole
